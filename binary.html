How to Use:

  1. Add Process: Click this button to create a new process (P1, P2, etc.). The process will immediately try to enter the Critical Section.
  2. Process in CS Finishes: When a process is inside the Critical Section, this button becomes active. Click it to simulate the process completing its work. This will trigger the signal() operation, potentially allowing another process to enter.

What's Happening Behind the Scenes?

  • wait() operation: When a process is added, it performs a wait(). If the semaphore is green (unlocked), it turns red (locked) and the process enters. If it's already red, the process is added to the Waiting Queue.
  • signal() operation: When you click "Process in CS Finishes", the process performs a signal(). If the Waiting Queue is not empty, the first process in line is allowed to enter. If the queue is empty, the semaphore turns green (unlocked).
